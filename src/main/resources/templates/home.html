Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data truly secure?
Absolutely. SCM is built on Spring Boot Security. Passwords are hashed using BCrypt, and database connections are fully encrypted. We never sell or expose your personal data.
Can I export my contacts later?
Yes! We offer 1-click exports. You can download your entire address book, or specific search query results, directly to Excel (.xlsx) or PDF formats.
Do you support OAuth login?
Yes, you can bypass the standard registration and securely log in using your existing Google or GitHub accounts.
What technologies power SCM 2.0?
SCM 2.0 is an enterprise-grade application powered by Java, Spring Boot, Hibernate, MySQL, Thymeleaf, and Tailwind CSS.
