Understanding the Process

What happens after I submit a feature request?
Feature requests are logged into our internal project tracker. The product team reviews them weekly during sprint planning. If your request aligns with the roadmap or receives high community demand, it gets scheduled for development.
How are bug reports prioritized?
Bugs marked as "High (Blocker)" involving data loss or security vulnerabilities are escalated immediately to on-call engineers. UI/UX glitches are typically patched in the next minor release cycle.
Will I receive an email response?
We send an automated confirmation receipt. A human response is sent only if the engineering team requires more specific steps to reproduce a bug, or to notify you when your requested feature is shipped.
